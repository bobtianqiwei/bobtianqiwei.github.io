Hook-Loop Units（贴贴）

Product Design
Individual work (academic)
Tianqi Wei
Tutor: Lintao Tang
10.2022-11.2022

Hook and Loop Fastener is a commonly used on luggage and clothing. It consist of two components: the first component features tiny hooks; the second features smaller loops. It can provide stable support in the direction parallel to the sticky surface, while it can be torn from the side and repeatedly sticky.

Each component only has one functional side (loop or hook).
‍
In Hook-Loop Units, I installed the loop and hook in a fabric on both sides, so it can be stuck with itself or with others.

‍

Hook-Loop Units offer endless possibilities because of their ability to stick to each other.
